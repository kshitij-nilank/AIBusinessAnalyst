# AIBusinessAnalyst

## Overview

AI Business Analyst is an internal AI assistant designed to understand Parcon's business domain and assist Business Analysts in handling reporting and ad-hoc requirements.

The objective is not simply to generate SQL, but to think like an experienced Business Analyst.

---

## Problem

Business Analysts spend significant time:

- Understanding stakeholder requirements
- Looking for previous SQL
- Modifying existing reports
- Understanding business rules
- Writing BigQuery SQL
- Reviewing SQL

A large amount of business knowledge exists only in people's experience.

---

## Solution

The AI Business Analyst learns:

- Company knowledge
- Database schema
- Business rules
- Reporting standards
- Stakeholder preferences
- SQL patterns

It then assists analysts by generating and reviewing SQL.

---

## MVP Scope

Version 1 includes:

- Requirement understanding
- Business knowledge
- SQL generation
- SQL review

Version 1 will NOT:

- Execute SQL
- Send emails
- Create dashboards
- Modify production systems
