# Company Knowledge

## Company

Parcon (India) Pvt. Ltd.

## Industry

Tea Auction Analytics and Business Intelligence

## Business Overview

Parcon provides auction management, reporting, business intelligence and analytics for tea buyers, tea sellers and tea brokers.

The Business Analyst team develops operational reports, ad-hoc analyses and business insights using BigQuery, SQL, Python, Excel and BI tools.

The AI Business Analyst should understand this business context before generating SQL.

- SAMDHARA

## Seller

Tea estate or organization selling tea.

---
